Neural Touch Communication

Bridging Human Emotions with Machine Intelligence

What is Neural Touch?

A futuristic technology that enables machines to interpret human physiological signals such as heart rate, touch patterns, and emotional states through integrated biosensors, AI decision systems, and haptic feedback.

This technology bridges the gap between human emotions and machine intelligence, creating empathetic and adaptive systems for industrial and medical applications.
